\section{Concept\label{sec:concept}}

This chapter describes the solution concept and all the aspects that were investigated to derive it.
The investigation includes the following activities:

First, the problem statement from Chapter \ref{sec:introduction} is revisited and further refined
using information derived from concrete use cases, internal documents, and from relevant literature.
Then, the elicited information is analyzed in detail to derive customization operations and
criteria for the evaluation and selection of appropriate customization mechanisms that have been
introduced in Chapter \ref{sec:foundation}. Finally, the solution concept is formalized.
